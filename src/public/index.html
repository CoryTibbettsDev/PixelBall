How To Play

Move you character with the arrow keys or WASD. Create a teleporter exit with E and an entrance with Q. If the ball passes through your entrance and you have an exit the ball with be teleported there. You can have one exit and up to two entrances. You can also create a barrier with R. If the ball hits it it will reverse direction horizontally. You spawn infront of your goal defend it while trying to score on your opponents, first to three wins.

About

PixelBall is an online multiplayer browser game designed to be competitive and fun. It took inspiration from pong, air hockey and other such games but puts it's own twist of the game by making the player indirectly affect the ball by creating teleporters and barriers instead of just blocking it themselves.
